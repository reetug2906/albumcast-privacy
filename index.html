AlbumCast

Privacy Policy · RAG Developers
Short version: AlbumCast does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your photos never leave your device — we only display them as a screensaver.

1. What AlbumCast Does

AlbumCast is an Android TV screensaver application that displays photos and videos from Google Photos shared albums as a slideshow when your TV enters screensaver mode.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

AlbumCast does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • We do not access your full Google Photos library
  • We do not track your usage, viewing habits, or preferences beyond local device storage
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties

3. How AlbumCast Works

AlbumCast works exclusively with publicly shared Google Photos album links that you explicitly provide. The app:

  • Fetches the publicly accessible shared album page from Google's servers
  • Extracts photo and video URLs from that public page
  • Displays those photos and videos locally on your TV
  • Stores only the album URL and your display preferences locally on your device

4. Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your Android TV device only and is never transmitted to our servers (we have no servers):

  • Google Photos shared album URLs you have added
  • Your screensaver preferences (interval, transition effects, clock display, etc.)

5. Internet Access

AlbumCast requires internet access solely to:

  • Fetch publicly shared Google Photos album pages to retrieve photo URLs
  • Download photos and videos for display as a screensaver

All network requests are made directly to Google's servers (lh3.googleusercontent.com and photos.app.goo.gl). No requests are made to any AlbumCast or third-party servers.

6. Google Photos

AlbumCast only accesses Google Photos content through public shared album links. We do not integrate with the Google Photos API and do not require any Google account sign-in. Your Google account credentials are never accessed or stored by AlbumCast.

7. Children's Privacy

AlbumCast does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features that would collect personal information from any user.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL. We will update the "Last Updated" date below accordingly.
